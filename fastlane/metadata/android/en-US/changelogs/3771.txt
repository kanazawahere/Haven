Port knocking: per-profile TCP/UDP knock sequence fired before connect.

Add a sequence like "7000 8000 9000" or "7000/tcp 8000/udp 9000/tcp" to any
SSH/Mosh/ET/VNC/RDP/SMB profile and Haven sends the packets at the remote
firewall (knockd, fwknop) right before the real socket open. Test knock button
verifies the sequence without connecting. Knock results land in the Connection
Log. Skipped on SSH-tunneled and SOCKS-routed paths — knock can't reach the
firewall from there.

MCP: new test_port_knock, set_port_knock, get_connection_log tools.
