• One more F-Droid build fix, found by running their build end to end rather than guessing at it. The Wayland stack's symbol-stub generator sorts two lists and compares them, and the two tools disagree about ordering unless the locale is C, so the build failed with an error after the library had already been built. It had never been seen because since v5.68.41 the F-Droid build died earlier and never reached that step. Nothing changes in the app itself.
