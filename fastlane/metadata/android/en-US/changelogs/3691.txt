v5.30.0 — WireGuard / Tailscale routing for non-SSH transports (#149)

Per-app WG / Tailscale tunnels now apply to every TCP transport: pick
a tunnel from "Route through" on any VNC, RDP, SMB, Reticulum, or
rclone profile. Multiple profiles sharing one tunnel share one
userspace handle, torn down on the last release. Legacy SOCKS5 /
SOCKS4 / HTTP proxies are also honoured for non-SSH transports now.

Smaller fixes: Desktop-tab Disconnect now reads as a ✕ icon (#148);
Reset Zoom too. README cleanup.
