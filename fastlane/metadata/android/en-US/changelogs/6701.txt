🖱️ **Terminal: touchpad two-finger scroll on some tablets** — on certain OEM tablets (reported on OPPO Pad 3 Pro) a Bluetooth-keyboard touchpad's two-finger scroll moved the app's own lists but did nothing in the terminal, because those drivers only send a scroll to a view that advertises itself as scrollable. The terminal now declares vertical scroll semantics, which should route the gesture through — and makes the terminal scrollable to TalkBack too. Candidate fix; feedback welcome.
