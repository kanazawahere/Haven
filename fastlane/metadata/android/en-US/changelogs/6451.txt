• F-Droid builds, take two. v5.68.62 was meant to unblock F-Droid and did not. The Wayland desktop stack needs a wayland-scanner that runs on the build machine and exactly matches our vendored Wayland; v5.68.62 began building one from our own source, but the cross build never actually used it — it kept resolving the build image's copy, the very thing that was breaking. Our scanner is now the one it finds, and a build image shipping a different version can no longer hijack the lookup. Nothing changes in the app itself.
